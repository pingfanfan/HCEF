About HCEF-LLM

Human-Centric Approach

Evaluates LLMs as potential partners rather than just tools, focusing on collaboration and trust-building capabilities.

Core Capability Dimensions

Six fundamental dimensions: Input Processing, Knowledge Retention, Logical Reasoning, Creative Cognition, Human-Centricity, and Output Generation.

Proficiency Descriptors

Four-level progression scale: Emergent, Developing, Proficient, and Expert, mapping the journey towards Ideal Intelligent Partners.

Framework Components

IIP Functional Model

  • Precise Instruction & Constraint Comprehension
  • Deep Goal & Intent Understanding
  • Capable & Adaptive Task Execution
  • Quality-Assured Outcome Delivery
